All About Coffee Cup Cafe

Welcome to Coffee Cup Cafe, where every cup tells a story and every sip is an experience. Nestled in the heart of St. John's, located at 170 Main Street we are newly established in 2024, our cozy and inviting coffee shop is a haven for coffee enthusiasts and those seeking a warm, inviting atmosphere.
At Coffee Cup Cafe, we take pride in serving the finest selection of premium fresh coffee beans, handcrafted by our skilled baristas. Whether you're a fan of rich espresso, creamy lattes, or indulgent flavored brews, our diverse menu has something to delight every palate.
Our commitment to quality extends beyond our beverages to our cozy ambiance, creating a welcoming space for friends, families, and solo coffee connoisseurs alike. Our store provides an authentic cafe experience, we also offer free wi-fi to our guests to ensure you can spend your time with us comfortably.
Come join us at Coffee Cup Cafe, where the love for coffee meets a comforting sense of community.
